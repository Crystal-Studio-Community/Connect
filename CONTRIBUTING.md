# 🛠️ Contributing to Crystal-Studio-Community Support (Connect)

Thank you for choosing to contribute to our onboarding and support repository! 👋 We are excited to have you build the gateway of our community with us.

---

## 🌟 How Can I Contribute?

### 1️⃣ Report Issues or Share Ideas 💡
If you have questions about the organization, find bugs in the auto-invite script, or want to suggest improvements to our profiles:
- Open a **[New Issue](https://github.com/Crystal-Studio-Community/Connect/issues/new)**.
- Use a clear title and description.

### 2️⃣ Submit Pull Requests (PRs) 🚀
1. **Fork** the repository and create your branch from `main`.
2. Commit your changes with clear messages and standard emojis!
3. Open a **Pull Request** and describe what your changes do.

Thank you for making Crystal-Studio-Community awesome! ❤️
